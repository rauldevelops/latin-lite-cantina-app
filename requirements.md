Latin Lite Cantina App Requirements

- Upload menu item recipes using ingredients with appropriate portions and measurements (nutritional information, inventory, and cost calculation)
- Upload individual ingredients with updated price, nutritional information, and quantity
- Dynamically update inventory with each customer order placed or group of orders batched
- Calculate food and container orders using historical inventory and order data
- User authentication (admin, cantina specialist, driver, customer)
- Create optimized delivery routes based on driver territories
- Dedicated driver dashboard where they can check off deliveries as they happen and make notes in real time
- Customer order module where customers can choose all of their mains and sides, or randomize, or base off dietary restrictions etc.
